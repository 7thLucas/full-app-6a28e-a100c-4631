Why WAKE UP?
😩 The Problem

Alarm apps are stressful & ugly

Most alarm apps greet you with harsh beeps, sterile interfaces, and zero personality. Starting your day is already hard — your alarm shouldn't make it worse. Users snooze endlessly or reach for their phone dreading the experience.

  • • Cold, clinical UI with no warmth or delight
  • • Boring analog/digital clocks with no character
  • • Nothing to look forward to when waking up
🦕 The Solution

A joyful dino companion to start your day

WAKE UP wraps your morning alarm in a warm, cheerful 3D world — a cute yellow dinosaur character, a sleepy-face analog clock, and a bright gradient UI that turns alarm-setting into something you actually enjoy doing.

  • • Expressive dino mascot that waves and helps set the clock
  • • Cute analog clock with sleepy face & personality
  • • Warm yellow gradient palette that energises, not irritates
